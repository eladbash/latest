Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Menu Bar Native

Lives in your tray, always accessible with a single click. Stays out of your way until you need it.

Multiple Sources

Checks Homebrew Casks, Sparkle feeds, and the Mac App Store. One place for all your updates.

One-Click Updates

Download and install directly from the app. No more hunting through websites or app stores.

How it works

Three steps. Zero hassle.

1

Scans your apps

Discovers every app in your Applications folder and identifies update sources.

2

Checks for updates

Queries Homebrew, Sparkle, and the Mac App Store on a configurable schedule.

3

Update with one click

See what's new and install updates directly — no extra steps needed.

Install

Up and running in seconds

$ curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/eladbash/latest/main/install.sh | sh
